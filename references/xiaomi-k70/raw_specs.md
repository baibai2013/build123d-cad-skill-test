# Redmi K70 Raw Specifications

## Data Sources
- GSMArena: dimensions confirmed 162.78 × 75.74 × 8.59 mm
- Xiaomi official: display 6.67" AMOLED 2K (3200×1440) 120Hz
- Multiple review sites cross-validated

## Body
- Height: 162.78 mm
- Width: 75.74 mm
- Thickness: 8.59 mm
- Weight: ~209 g
- Frame: aluminum alloy mid-frame
- Back: glass (Corning Gorilla Glass Victus)
- Front: Corning Gorilla Glass Victus
- IP68 dust/water resistant

## Display
- Size: 6.67 inches
- Resolution: 3200 × 1440 (2K)
- Type: AMOLED, 120Hz
- Flat screen (non-curved / 直屏)
- Center punch-hole front camera

## Camera (Rear)
- Layout: rectangular module, upper-left of back panel
- Main: 50 MP, Sony IMX882, f/1.6, OIS
- Ultrawide: 8 MP, f/2.2
- Macro: 2 MP, f/2.4
- Flash: LED, within camera module area

## Camera (Front)
- 16 MP, center punch-hole at top

## Buttons (Right Side)
- Volume rocker: upper position
- Power button: below volume, integrated fingerprint sensor

## Bottom
- USB Type-C port: center
- Speaker grille: right of USB-C
- SIM card tray: left of USB-C
- Microphone hole

## Top
- IR blaster
- Secondary microphone
